<h3>Steamed Veggies Medley</h3>
<p>If you're looking to get more greens into your diet, this veggie medley is a perfect choice. Plus, it takes under 10 minutes to prepare!</p>
<h4><a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Food+Steamer&tag=github-automation-20">Check Pricing in Amazon</a></h4><br><br><ul>
  <li><strong>Ingredients:</strong> 1 cup of broccoli florets, 1 cup of sliced carrots, 1 cup of cauliflower florets, salt, pepper, and a dash of olive oil.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Instructions:</strong></p>
<ol>
  <li>Place all the veggies in your microwave food steamer.</li>

<h3>Quick Quinoa Pilaf</h3>
<p>Quinoa is a great source of protein and cooks beautifully in a microwave food steamer. Try this flavorful quinoa pilaf as a side dish or a light meal.</p>
<h4><a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Food+Steamer&tag=github-automation-20">Check Pricing in Amazon</a></h4><br><br><ul>
  <li><strong>Ingredients:</strong> 1 cup quinoa, 2 cups vegetable broth, 1 chopped bell pepper, 1 small onion, and your choice of herbs (like thyme or oregano).</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Instructions:</strong></p>
<ol>
  <li>Rinse the quinoa under cold water in a strainer.</li>
  <li>In your microwave food steamer, mix the quinoa, broth, chopped bell pepper, onion, and herbs.</li>
  <li>Cover and cook on high for 10-12 minutes.</li>
  <li>Let it sit covered for an additional 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.</li>
</ol>

  <li><strong>Ingredients:</strong> 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast, 1 cup broccoli florets, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Instructions:</strong></p>
<ol>
