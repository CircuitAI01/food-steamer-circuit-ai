<p>Did you know that you can also make perfectly fluffy rice in your food steamer? This method is easy and yields amazing results. Here’s how:</p>
<ol>
    <li>Rinse one cup of rice under cold water until the water runs clear.</li>
    <li>Add the rinsed rice to the steamer basket.</li>
    <li>Pour two cups of water into the bowl.</li>
    <li>Set your steamer, and let it cook for about 20 minutes.</li>
</ol>
<p>Your rice will turn out soft and fluffy, making it the perfect base for any dish!</p>

    <li>Place it in the steamer basket and add lemon slices on top.</li>
    <li>Steam for about 10-15 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.</li>
</ul>
<p>This method not only cooks the fish perfectly but also infuses it with a lovely, fresh flavor.</p>
