<h3>Enhances Nutrient Retention</h3>
<p>One of the most significant benefits of steaming food is its ability to keep nutrients intact. Unlike boiling, which can cause vitamins and minerals to leach into the water, steaming cooks food using the steam produced from boiling water. This method helps maintain up to 90% of vitamins and minerals in your meals. Vegetables, especially, benefit from this method as they retain their vibrant colors and essential nutrients.</p>
<h3>Promotes Healthy Cooking</h3>
<p>With a food steamer pan, you can prepare meals with less oil or fat compared to other cooking methods like frying or sautéing. Steamed vegetables and proteins, such as fish or chicken, come out tender and flavorful without the need for excessive butter or oil. This makes it easier to control calorie intake while still enjoying delicious meals.</p>
