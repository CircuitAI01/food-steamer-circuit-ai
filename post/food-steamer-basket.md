<h3>Retains Nutrients</h3>
<p>One of the standout benefits of using a food steamer basket is its ability to preserve nutrients. When you boil vegetables, many of their vitamins and minerals seep into the water. Steaming, on the other hand, keeps these nutrients intact. Here’s how:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Steaming requires minimal water, which means fewer nutrients are lost.</li>
    <li>The cooking temperature is gentler, making it easier for vegetables to maintain their vibrant colors and essential minerals.</li>
    <li>Vitamins like C and B, which are sensitive to heat and water, stay locked in your food.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Easy to Use</h3>
<p>Another benefit of a food steamer basket is its simplicity. You don’t need to have advanced cooking skills to use it effectively. Here's the basic process:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Fill your pot with water.</li>
    <li>Place the steamer basket on top, making sure it doesn’t touch the water.</li>
    <li>Add your food, cover, and steam for the recommended time based on what you're cooking.</li>
</ul>
<p>That's it! You can set a timer or keep an eye on things while you multitask in the kitchen.</p>
