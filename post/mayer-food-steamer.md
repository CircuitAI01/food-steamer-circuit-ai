<h3>Descaling Your Steamer</h3>
<p>If you live in an area with hard water, mineral buildup can occur inside your Mayer food steamer. Descaling your steamer regularly is crucial.</p>
<h4><a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Food+Steamer&tag=github-automation-20">Check Pricing in Amazon</a></h4><br><br><p>To descale:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Mix equal parts of water and distilled white vinegar.</li>
    <li>Fill the water reservoir with this mixture.</li>
    <li>Run the steamer on a high setting for about 20-30 minutes.</li>
    <li>Rinse thoroughly with clean water afterward to remove any vinegar taste or smell.</li>
</ul>
